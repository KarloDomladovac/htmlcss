Referral process

1. In order to make a referral, please go to the menu and select ‘Invite a friend’. In addition to the number of referrals you have remaining, you will also find your personal invitation code.

2. To copy your invitation code simply click on it or select one of the ways you want to share your code with your friends.

3. You can send your invitation code to the person you want to refer via Email, Whatsapp or any social media platform.

4. As soon as your referral receives the code, it can be used on the BestSecret login screen under ‘Registration’.

5. After entering all necessary personal data and accepting the T&C, your referral will receive a final confirmation email. This will be sent to the entered email address.

6. The registration process is complete, as soon as the account is verified via the link in the confirmation email. Please also check your spam folder, if you are unable to find the email. In rare cases, it can take a few hours until the confirmation email arrives.
